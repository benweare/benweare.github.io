Hello! I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Nottingham.

Current Research

My focus is adapting transmission electron microscopes (TEMs) into electron diffractometers, in order to solve the structures of crystals too small for X-ray techniques. More generally I am involved in developing non-standard operating procedures for conventional TEMs, such as low-voltage alignment, and writing scripts to streamline TEM data processing.
