Biography

I finished my Master's and PhD in Chemistry at the University of Nottingham, where I learned to use a TEM.

Research Expertise

Current research is focussed on the development of electron diffraction for structure solution at the Nanoscale and Microscale Research Centre (nmRC).

PhD research included method application of tranmission electron microscopy (TEM) techniques to covalent organic frameworks (COFs).

Master's research included the synthesis and halogenation of hexazatrinapthylene (HATN) moleules, followed by investigations into their electron beam stability.
